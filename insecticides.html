Insecticides

These are synthetic chemicals used to manage insects' pests. They are formulated to kill, harm, repel or mitigate one or more species of insects. They work in different capacities, some disrupt their nervous system, others may damage their exoskeletons, repel them or even control them by some other means. They can be applied through sprays, dusts, gels, and even baits.

Insecticides are used in many settings and applications. Insecticides have been claimed to be the biggest factor behind the advancement realized in agriculture and also a large influence on 20th century agricultural productivity.
