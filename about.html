About Us

A leading importer and distributor of agrochemicals, fertilizers and farm inputs in Uganda.

Our Story

Serving Uganda's Farmers with Trusted Inputs

Uganda Crop Care Limited (UCCL) – a fully registered company in Uganda situated at Bata Compound, Plot-92/94, 5th Street Industrial area, Kampala – is a leading importer, and distributor of agrochemicals, fertilizers and other farm inputs in Uganda. Our supply of products meet the highest standards in production, quality, safety and service.

We use our profound market know-how for having better access to business opportunities with selected partners within the entire value-creation chain. Our success is based on our flexible, reliable and expert team.

Why Choose Us

Our Strengths

Ethical and well respected company in Uganda for the last decade

Experienced management team & highly qualified techno-commercial personnel

Strong partnerships with suppliers including multinationals in agribusiness

Excellent financial track record and capabilities

Proven track record in distribution of a wide range of products

What We Offer

Our Products

UCCL offer a wide range of agrochemicals that prevent the plants from insecticide, fungicide, herbicide, acaricide, agricultural chemicals for easy and quick growth of plants and thus plant growth regulators and stimulants are essential. We supply fertilizers and foliar fertilizers that give nutrition to crops and help plants develop tolerance against pests like weeds, insects and diseases.
