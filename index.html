Our Products

Premium Agro-Chemical Solutions

Fertilizers

Fertilizers are substances containing chemical elements such as manure or mixture of nitrates that improves the growth of plants. They give nutrition to the crops. When added to soil or water, plants can develop tolerance against pests like weeds, insects and diseases.

View Products

Chemicals

UCCL offer a wide range of agrochemicals that prevent the plants from insecticide, fungicide, herbicide, acaricide, agricultural chemicals for easy and quick growth of plants and thus plant growth regulators and stimulants are essential.

View Products

Foliar Fertilizers

Foliar fertilizer technology is a unique, dynamic and effective method of crop nutrition. Foliar feeding provides an excellent solution when the plant root system is not functioning optimally or nutrition via the soil is malfunctioning.
